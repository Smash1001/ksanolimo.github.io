About

Hey, I’m Kai 👋

I’m a 23-year-old recent graduate from San Jose State University, where I studied International Business with a specialization in Global Operations Management. My professional interests sit at the intersection of global logistics, supply chain systems, and marketing — fields where strategy meets execution on a world stage.

As you explore this site, you’ll find a collection of projects spanning internships, coursework, and elected leadership roles. Whether it’s building vendor evaluation frameworks, supporting pharmaceutical marketing campaigns, or coordinating cross-border logistics, I bring the same approach to everything: show up, learn fast, and add value.

I’m an extrovert at heart and thrive in cross-functional environments where different perspectives come together to solve real problems.

A Little More About Me

  • 🌏 Japanese speaker (intermediate) — I’ve been to Japan 10+ times, and during my study abroad at Sophia University in Tokyo, I somehow ended up working shifts at a falafel shop. No regrets.
  • 🇰🇭 Half Chinese-Cambodian — I’ve visited Cambodia three times and hope to deepen that connection over time, both personally and professionally.
  • 📰 News reader — always trying to stay sharp on what’s happening in global markets and geopolitics.
  • 🏋️ Afternoon gym sessions — consistency over intensity.
  • 🗺️ Side quest enthusiast — if there’s a new food spot within reasonable driving distance, I’m probably already looking it up.
